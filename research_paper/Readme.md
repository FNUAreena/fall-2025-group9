# Overview

The research paper presents the entire project in a clear, academically structured format. 

It highlights:

- The motivation behind the study, including the need to reduce food waste and improve school meal operations
- The full data pipeline, from raw HTML extraction to preprocessing and feature engineering
- A comparative evaluation of Machine Learning and Deep Learning forecasting models
- Experimental findings for both univariate and multivariate cost prediction
- Practical implications for FCPS meal planning and operational decision-making
- Key limitations of the current approach and directions for future work
